Welcome

Dear Valued User,

Welcome to Blood Frontier, and thanks for your interest! Please note that Blood Frontier is in early Beta stages, and as such, is not yet fully finished or polished. It is intended as a multiplayer only demo of what is to come in future versions, meaning that singleplayer and coop are not implemented yet. You are however invited to try out the features that have been added that will lead up to these things, such as; bots, online triggers, and much more :)

If you are interested in development, or require technical support please visit the IRC channel on irc.freenode.net in #bloodfrontier or you can visit our website, which offers more details at www.bloodfrontier.com. Enjoy messing around with our little project, and be sure to have fun, because we do!

Regards,

The Blood Frontier Team
